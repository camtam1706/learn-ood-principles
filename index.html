🛠️ Low Level Design Interview Using OOD Principles

Your personalized roadmap for mastering Object-Oriented Design interviews

📅 WEEK 1

✅ Introduction

This chapter introduces the course, including its prerequisites, structure, strengths, and the target audience.
🟩 Cornerstones of Object-oriented Programming

Covers the background of OOP: encapsulation, abstraction, inheritance, and polymorphism, ending with a quiz on basics.
🧩 Object-oriented Design

Explore how to conduct object-oriented analysis and design using UML, use case, class, sequence, and activity diagrams.
📐 Object-oriented Design Principles

Covers the SOLID design principles: Single Responsibility, Open Closed, Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, and Dependency Inversion.
🎯 Design Patterns

Learn the classification of design patterns: creational, structural, and behavioral patterns. Includes a quiz.
🌏 Real-world Design Problems

Discover how to approach and solve real-world design problems using object-oriented principles and methodologies.
🅿️ Designing a Parking Lot

Covers the design of a parking lot system, requirements, UML diagrams, and implementation code to illustrate practical application.
